FAQS

¿What is Bimbo Global Race?

Bimbo Global Race is the largest ‘Race with a Cause’ in the world. It has been running for over 8 editions until 2023. This year, you will be able to run it both physically or virtually in more than 34 countries around the globe. Our goal is to feed a better world, so we’ll donate 20 slices of bread for every subscribed runner on the physical race and 2 slices of bread for every new user subscribed to our community. So far, we have donated more than 16 million slices of bread. We are sure that this year we’ll surpass a new goal. And we want to see you there!

Who organizes Bimbo Global Race?

Grupo Bimbo, the largest bread maker in the world, does, as one of the actions to take in order to become an even more sustainable, productive and wholesomely human enterprise. Not only we produce delicious and nurturing food for the whole family, we go far beyond. If you want to know more about Grupo Bimbo, click here: https://www.grupobimbo.com/

Will there be a physical race this year?

Yes! There will be a physical race on sunday, September 24th*. You can check out the list of the countries where a physical race is taking place here and also join our community to be updated on every news about the race. A great experience in every mile. We’ll expect to see you there.

*In Argentina the physical race will take place on September 10th and in Spain on September 17th.

Are there categories within the race?

Tha BGR has 3km, 5km and 10km, each with male and female categories*.
*Categories may vary according to the country

Are there any kits for virtual racers?

Sadly no. Kits and all other benefits are just for runners of the physical BGR 2023.

How can I register on the BGR 2023?

It 's so easy! You just have to enter www.bimboglobalrace.com, write down your info and give us your email in order to start receiving all of our notifications. Don’t forget that being part of the Bimbo Global Race team, gives you tons of benefits.

Will there be any prizes this year?

1st place winners of the 10km race (male and female) will be granted with the right to run on the host city of the Bimbo Global Race 2024, with an all inclusive* package for sightseeing and city tour*. Are you ready to run? See you at the Bimbo Global Race 2023.
*Consult terms and conditions about the prize.
